Assa-North Ohaji South Project

The Assa North-Ohaji South project (ANOH) stands as one of the largest greenfield gas condensate development endeavors in Nigeria.

This initiative entails the advancement of the Ohaji South gas and condensate field within the license block OML 53, along with the Assa North field in license block OML 21.

Collectively, these two fields are anticipated to yield 600 million standard cubic feet per day (Mscfd) of gas, equating to around 2.4GW of electricity. This electricity generation capacity is ample to meet the needs of approximately 2.4 million households.
